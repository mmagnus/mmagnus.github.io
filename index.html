SimRNAweb

SimRNA is a method for RNA 3D structure modeling with optional restraints. SimRNA uses (1) a coarse-grained representation, (2) relies on the Monte Carlo method for sampling the conformational space, and (3) employs a statistical potential to approximate the energy and identify conformations that correspond to biologically relevant structures.
